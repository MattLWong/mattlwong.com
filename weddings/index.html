Hello! I'm Matt, a wedding and engagement photographer based in the San Francisco Bay Area, California. As your photographer, my goal is quite simply to document your wedding through a powerful combination of simple and elegant imagery that brings you right into the scene. (Read more...)

My style focuses on capturing the elements of joy, serendipity, and humor. With two years of experience as a studio portrait photographer, I'm keenly aware of how people want to be seen and what angles work best. I bring my knowledge about shooting in natural, artificial and hybrid lighting condition, which means I can provide technical solutions for any venue of your choosing.

On the creative side, I love the grainy, saturated look of film. Yet digital cameras have come a long way in terms of reliability, portability, and speed. With this in mind, I shoot digital and post-process the final images to have the timeless look of film.

I became a wedding photographer quite unexpectedly. I've been enamoured by photography since I was very young, but the close partnership between the photographer and married couple is uniquely intimate and something that I have come to deeply value. It is my ultimate privilege and pleasure to be with you on your important day and document your story through timeless images.
