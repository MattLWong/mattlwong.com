Hello! My name is Matt, and I'm a professional wedding and portrait photographer based in the San Francisco Bay Area with a longstanding interest in historical portraiture. As your photographer my mission is to capture key moments of your wedding through elegant and timeless imagery that transcends the boundaries of daily life. Weddings are a culmination of joy and togetherness, and it is my privilege as your photographer to preserve the memories of your important day for years to come.
